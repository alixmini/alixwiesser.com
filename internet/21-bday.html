TURNING 21

The first video of an annual video I now make for my birthday. I'm still getting used to my birthday falling in the winter, which makes it feel more sad than it did growing up in perpetual summer.

I film a few moments every time I think to take out my camera or phone (it depends on the year), then I overlay a song that feels correct. They are simple, they are nostalgic. I very much love this tradition.
