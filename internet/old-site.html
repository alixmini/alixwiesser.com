THE OLD WEBSITE

I have always loved lists. From the age of six, I would fill my notebooks with lists of my favourite songs; all the words I knew in French; what I would buy for groceries when I moved out at 18, which included this definitively; the names of all of my friends.

Because of this, I also started a lot of blogs at around that age, some of which I can't find for the life of me, and others which you can find on this very website. I liked and still like the idea of consolidating my identity in lists and a .com felt like the perfect place for that. At the time, that was blogspot.com, but now I like to tend to my own domain.

I used Claude to make the first version of alixwiesser.com in late 2024. The main goal was to get it live, and the fun bits were the non-negotiables. Going from monochromatic "Mini mode" to colourful, floaty bouncing DVD logo-inspired "Maxi mode" is a feature I was quite pleased with.

I try not to talk about being a massive Harry Potter fan in middle school, but it slips out occasionally. This website was one such implicit case, because you could translate the entire site to Parseltongue by way of an AI-created 'translation'. Hell yeah.
