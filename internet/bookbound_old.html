bookbound

Bookbound is a webapp I spun up pre-vibecoding era as part of buildspace in 2024. I had spent the summer in Berkeley freelancing and learning a bit of design, enjoying the sun. Like any bright‑eyed UX course‑taker, I had the brilliant idea to redesign Goodreads’ notoriously user‑unfriendly app (lol). A few days into this I became bored and also felt that the project didn’t match the spirit of buildspace or of my brain. Instead I changed course and built Bookbound, which I called a “Spotify Wrapped but for your reading”. I used the Google Books API because, as previously mentioned, using anything Goodreads‑related would be a massive faff. The video demo I posted to Twitter is a nice, quick walkthrough of how it works.

I didn’t renew the domain BUT I did write about the process on Substack; it’s honestly a bit outdated, especially since the advent of Cursor and Claude Code. Very cool to see how fast things have moved and how novel the process felt up until recently.
