THIS MADE ME THINK OF U

"this made me think of u" is what you text someone to show them that you care. You're thinking of them, you're remembering them, you're letting them know. But you can't always let people know, for whatever reason. So where does that text go?

tmmtou.com was a 2-day project I vibecoded on a whim: you upload a photo, write a message, and sign off (or stay anonymous). The idea was heavily inspired by the ongoing community mail project PostSecret, and other similar projects.

The upload box has a zig-zagging border like that of a postage stamp. The uploaded photos lay skewed on the page as you scroll. It's bittersweet to scroll through, but it's mostly sweet.
