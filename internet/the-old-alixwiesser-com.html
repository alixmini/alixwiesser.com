I have always loved lists. From the age of six, I would fill my notebooks with lists of my favourite songs; all the words I knew in French; what I would buy for groceries when I moved out at 18; the names of all of my friends. Because of this, I also started a lot of blogs at around that age, some of which I can't find for the life of me, and others which you can find on this very website. I liked and still like the idea of consolidating my identity in lists and a .com felt like the perfect place for that. Thus, the first version of alixwiesser.com was born!
