Christmas hamper

December, 2025. A new term: “grandma‑maxxing,” coined (afaik) by yours truly. In an effort to be more neighbourly — especially as the only 20‑somethings living in the building — I made some festive goodie bags for my neighbours and coworkers. I’m going to list out the process in case you’d like to do something similar: 01. Come up with a menu (mostly non‑ or semi‑perishables). I went with: a. Pear & cardamom shrub for festive drinks b. Coconut, cashew, and chocolate granola c. A jar of pickles d. Pannetone(ish) biscotti (orange zest glaze). 02. Acquire ingredients. 03. Acquire vessels & packaging. 04. Put on your apron and Christmas music. 05. Design menu card and write a personal message on the back. 06. Package everything! 07. Hand‑deliver your goods. You are now a grandma! Happy holidays! :)
