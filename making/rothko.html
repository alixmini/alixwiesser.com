DIY ROTHKO

This is not a Rothko painting, if that wasn't already obvious. But you might see the resemblance, which is good, because I was going for Rothko-inspired.

The idea was that everybody says "how do those paintings sell for millions? I could do that!". In fact, most of us could not and never will. To prove this, I went on Google Images and recreated the pink-est Rothko I could find, named "Red and Pink on Pink, c.1953". I only had acrylic paint, and (see: posters) I have never been much of a painter, but I made do. There is certainly a resemblance, albeit a very weak one! I quite like how it looks on my mantelpiece.

Now, when people come over, I point to it and say "that's my Rothko". I would be willing to ship it to the highest bidder, though, so hit me up if you think that could be you.
