PAINTING(S)

In an effort to undo the idea that art is just not my thing (isn't it funny how we decide who we are before the age of eight?), I've gotten into painting, often with wine present and sometimes with friends. It's meditative, it's fun, and you have a pretty piece of art at the end.

They look cool and are uniquely mine, especially as they rarely end up being similar to the inspiration pieces I pull off Pinterest. As of this update, my latest painting is the blue Chinese girl in the cheongsam. Her pupils are clementines and she has a septum piercing. Very dope.
