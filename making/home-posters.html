Home posters

Everyone wants posters until they start looking for posters. I want my walls to be filled with things I love! My walls should be a reflection of my me‑ness. Redbubble will not do. Amazon certainly will not. My pockets are not nearly deep enough for the most epic posters. I wanted my walls to feel like home, though — so I make and source posters that mean something to me, rather than buying the default options.
