lighter magnets

When you visit any souvenir store, you’re guaranteed the basics: mugs, postcards, placemats. Depending on where you are, you may also come across near‑propagandistic paraphernalia and even lewd apparel, if you’re lucky. A friend of mine had a really excellent magnet collection — so much so that she would keep buying new IKEA magnet boards because she ran out of space multiple times. Inspired by this, I started a souvenir collection of my own in 2021 and went with pocket lighters. I’m fairly indiscriminate when it comes to choosing a lighter, and I enjoy the little narratives they bring.
