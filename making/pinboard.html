Pinboard

My mum thinks I’m a hoarder, but I actually just really love holding onto stuff. I had a box of mementos that almost accidentally got thrown away, but thankfully, they ended up being thrown-up onto my wall. I bought a big sticky corkboard sheet off Amazon and let three layers of pink acrylic paint dry before pinning up my most favourite memories: boarding passes, birthday cards, restaurant receipts, photobooth strips, postcards. It’s a living, breathing thing that you can add to and edit as often as feels right.
