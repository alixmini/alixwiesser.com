While I was working at tldraw, the team had been building Teach, an experiment in giving LLMs full control and flexibility over canvas-based interactions, in an effort to test whether we can move AI interaction away from traditional chat-based interfaces. While I didn’t play any role in creating Teach, I did spend a lot of time playing with it and all its quirks (of which there are many). Lu and I did a lightning demo at RAG++ — The London AI Event — held at Frameless, which was an awesome space. tldraw is cool because it’s a product of real craftsmanship, but it also takes itself quite lightly insofar as brand voice and tone.
