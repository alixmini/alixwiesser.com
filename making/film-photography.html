FILM PHOTOGRAPHY

You can find my film pics on tumblr!

I bought my first camera at Clock Tower Cameras in Brighton on my twenty-third birthday, a little Pentax Espio for sixty-odd sterling. It's a 35mm point-and-shoot and I adore it.

Writing this has made me realise I haven't named her, so I'm taking ten seconds to stare out the window and christen her. Okay, nice! We're going with Margot.

Margot and I go through a roll of 36 photos in about 5 or 6 weeks on average, and get the negatives developed at Aperture Printing on Rathbone Place, just off Tottenham Court Road. Richard, the man who is always there when I go, is patient and discerning. I think they do something special in their lab to make the quality come out so beautifully.

With that being said, I'd love to have my own darkroom one day, or somehow fashion a makeshift one! …If that sounds fun to you, message me! Everything's more fun with a co-conspirator.
