LIGHTER MAGNET BOARD

When you visit any souvenir store, you're guaranteed the basics: mugs, postcards, placemats. Depending on where you are, you may also come across near-propagandistic paraphernalia and even lewd apparel, if you're lucky.

A friend of mine had a really excellent magnet collection, so much so that she would keep buying new IKEA magnet boards because she ran out of space multiple times. Inspired by this, I started a souvenir collection of my own in 2021 and went with pocket lighters. I'm fairly indiscriminate when it comes to choosing a lighter, I'll usually just go with whichever feels the kitschiest.

The point of souvenirs, though - and it's in the name - is that you want to remember them! The problem with collecting loose pocket lighters is that they will end up in a ziploc under your bed, or piled on top of one another in a nice glass dish. Turning them into magnets by way of hot glue-gunning little magnets to the back of the lighters. The result is two magnetic strips stuck to my wardrobe full of travel memories!
