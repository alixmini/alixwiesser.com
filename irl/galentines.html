GALENTINE'S 2025

Hosting dinner parties is one of life's greatest, greatest joys. You plan a menu, you do the prep, set the table, dress up. Your friends help you load the dishwasher! You drink wine! I love a dinner party, and this one on February 14th, 2025, was by far the best one yet.

Also, Tara and Gabby just so happened to be in London! I found out that two friends (who I met on exchange in California!) were visiting from New York, which was excellent timing. Everyone says this, I know, but it really is cool to have friends who you can go ages without seeing, and then pick up again like no time has passed at all.

On the menu: heart-shaped vodka pasta, burrata salad, olives, other snacky things.
And for dessert: Layered raspberry, rosewater, and lychee parfaits.

Highlights of the night included a dramatic reading of Becca's journal, and the fact that pretty much everything Audrey said that night was funny. Galentine's!
