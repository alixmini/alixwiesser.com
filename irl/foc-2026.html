I presented the Cottage for the first time at Feeling of Computing in February. It's a great event that I've posted about before on my website, and I highly recommend signing up as soon as the event goes live (it fills up fast!) if you're (ever) in London.

Watching people over their shoulder as they play through is incredibly entertaining, both because I predicted and failed to predict people's behaviour at different points in the Cottage. The file, written in Python, is called cottage.py, which I find highly amusing as well. Cottage pie.

I'm currently writing a blogpost on why I built the Cottage, and what work like this could mean for the future of AI in education and childhood learning development. This will be up on my Substack :)
