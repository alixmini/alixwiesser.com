Krish and Adlee asked if I wanted to help host the first installment of a series of daytime "cafés" at our friend Kristof's flat. This sounded fun, so I agreed. We named it 'London = good' in reference to the London —> SF brain drain our city is battling.

I made a lot of cakes the day before, and drank an insane amount of coffee on the day itself. If I recall correctly, there were about twenty people who milled around throughout the afternoon, so I'd say the café was a success and friends were made! I believe Adlee has since hosted at least four or five of these at the time of writing, which is great.

Sonja wrote about the café on her blog, which you should definitely read for London-in-your-20s tips. Quick aside: the podcast Sonja mentioned is Binchtopia, which is an excellent/funny podcast, although sadly, one of the hosts has since left the show to pursue her music career.
