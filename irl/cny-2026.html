CNY OF THE HORSE 2026

An early celebration for the Year of the Horse! This was the second year we hosted Chinese New Year at the flat, but with a lot of new faces! There weren't enough dumplings, but we did have enough Tsingtao, so it balances out.

We (well, I, much to the dismay of my flatmate who has heard it about 100 times in the past few weeks) played Kung Fu Fighting on the speakers about seven times. Excellent tune.

Apparently, everybody is in a Very Chinese Time in their lives, so energy was high. I have a good feeling about entering the Year of the Horse.
