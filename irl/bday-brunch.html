23RD BDAY BRUNCH

Brunch with friends, 23rd birthday edition! I was genuinely a little bit possessed, for lack of a better word, when it came to preparing the food for this gathering. I did the vast majority of the prep the day before, but thankfully, Becca saved the day and helped make everything look extra beautiful on the day itself - I love you!!!

In the photos, you are looking at from-scratch:

  • Whipped feta and olive oil dip, roasted romano peppers
  • Beetroot tzatziki, turmeric-pickled courgettes
  • Baba ganoush, lots of pomegranate seeds
  • Coriander-cannelini purée, charred corn ribs
  • Couscous salad, merguez sausages (hard to find in London, apparently!)
  • Pimm's and lemonade !!!

And for the birthday cake, a gluten-free orange-cardamom olive oil cake, because Caitlin is coeliac, so it was very beautiful but I probably needed more fat to make up for all that gluten-free flour lacks. Served with an earl-grey infused heavy cream. Dessert should never be an afterthought.

A gorgeous celebration, followed by a rather eventful evening, but that's a story for another time!
