RAG++ AI DEMO

While I was working at tldraw, the team had been building Teach, an experiment in giving LLMs full control and flexibility over canvas-based interactions, in an effort to test whether we can move AI interaction away from traditional chat-based interfaces. While I didn't play any role in creating Teach, I did spend a lot of time playing with it and all its quirks (of which there are many).

Lu and I did a lightning demo at RAG++ The London AI Event (which was held at Frameless), which was an awesome space, although I was close to throwing up before going on stage - likely due to a mix of nerves and the immersive graphics.

tldraw is cool because it's a product of real craftsmanship, but it also takes itself quite lightly insofar as brand voice and its presentation on socials go. It was fun to recreate that on stage, and I'll let you in on a secret: the blackberry joke was scripted.
